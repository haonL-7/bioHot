Evaluation Methodology

The BioHot evaluation framework is based on two methodological manuscripts by Liu Haoan and Feng Wen, Yulin University:

MS1 — Liu H, Feng W. "Compartmentalized Co-metabolism in the Porcine Gut: Systematic Evidence Mapping and a Theoretical Framework for Epigenetic–Microbiome Interactions" (2025–2026)
MS2 — Liu H, Feng W. "A Tiered Evidence Framework for Assessing Translational Readiness of Candidate Probiotics in Livestock: A Systematic Evidence Mapping with Methodological Development" (2025–2026)

The evaluation engine is powered by the DeepSeek API, performing structured evidence rating on newly indexed papers daily. All AI-generated assessments include confidence indicators and evaluation method transparency. Independent verification is recommended before citing.
